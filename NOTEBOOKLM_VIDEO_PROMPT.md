# NotebookLM Video Generation Prompt

## Prompt for NotebookLM Video Generation

Use this prompt in NotebookLM to generate a professional video script about the Inventory Store application:

---

**PROMPT:**

Create a compelling 3-5 minute video script for a promotional/explainer video about "Inventory Store" - a comprehensive inventory management and e-commerce solution. The video should:

1. **Opening (30 seconds)**
   - Hook viewers with a problem statement: "Running a store or restaurant? Struggling with inventory management, supplier coordination, and order processing?"
   - Introduce Inventory Store as the all-in-one solution

2. **Main Features Overview (2-3 minutes)**
   - Highlight key functionalities:
     - Complete e-commerce storefront with product catalog, shopping cart, and checkout
     - Real-time inventory management with automated stock tracking
     - Supplier and purchase order management system
     - Order pipeline from placement to delivery
     - Financial management with cost tracking and profit analysis
     - WhatsApp integration for instant order notifications
     - Multi-store support for growing businesses

3. **Benefits for Store Owners (1 minute)**
   - All-in-one platform eliminating need for multiple tools
   - Automated reorder suggestions preventing stockouts
   - Time savings through automation
   - Professional online presence
   - Complete audit trail for transparency

4. **Benefits for Restaurant Owners (1 minute)**
   - Ingredient tracking and inventory management
   - Menu item management with stock-based availability
   - Supplier management for food items
   - Cost and profitability analysis
   - Reduced food waste through better planning

5. **Closing (30 seconds)**
   - Emphasize production-grade technology and scalability
   - Call to action: "Transform your business operations today"
   - Include key differentiators: modern technology, complete audit trail, mobile-friendly, cost-effective

**Tone:** Professional, clear, and engaging. Use visual descriptions for what should appear on screen during each section.

**Target Audience:** Store owners and restaurant owners looking for inventory management solutions.

**Style:** Modern, tech-forward, business-focused explainer video with clear value propositions.
